🔒 Privacy Policy

Gravity Path – DUNA Studio

Effective April 21, 2026
Summary: We do not collect any personal information. Gravity Path is an offline game. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Gravity Path does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or any other identifying details.

The only data stored is your game progress (unlocked levels, high scores, selected skin) — all saved locally on your device via UserDefaults and never transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Google may collect certain device data to serve relevant ads in accordance with their own privacy policy.

  • Gravity Path does not share personal data with Google beyond what the AdMob SDK collects automatically.
  • Users in the EU/EEA will see a consent screen as required by GDPR.
  • Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

3. Device Permissions

  • Internet Access: Used only to load AdMob ads. The game runs fully offline.
  • Haptics: Tactile feedback when the ball hits a barrier — no data collected.
  • Audio: In-game sound effects — no recording, no microphone access.

4. Children

Gravity Path is suitable for all ages 4+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Ads are configured in family-safe mode.

5. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms. We have no ability to access this data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
