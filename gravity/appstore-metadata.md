## 📝 MÔ TẢ (English)

**Gravity Path** is a unique physics puzzle game where you tilt barriers to guide a rolling ball into the goal bucket!

🎯 **SIMPLE TO LEARN, HARD TO MASTER**
Tap to release the ball, swipe to rotate barriers, fine-tune angles to bounce and slide the ball through each stage into the bucket. One wrong angle and — SMASH!

🌍 **8 WORLDS TO EXPLORE**
From underground caves to outer space, Mars and beyond — each world introduces new mechanics and visual themes.

🧱 **3 BARRIER MATERIALS**
Wood (standard friction), Glass (ultra-slippery), Rubber (bouncy chaos!)

✨ **FEATURES**
• 35+ hand-crafted levels + infinite procedural generation
• Realistic physics engine
• Epic ball shatter effect on miss
• 5 collectible ball skins
• Score, records & 3-star rating
• Haptics & sound effects
• Vietnamese & English auto-detect
• Full offline play
