## 📝 DESCRIPTION (English US — max 4,000 characters)

**Duna's Gravity Path** is a unique physics puzzle game where you tilt barriers to guide a rolling ball into the goal bucket!

🎯 **SIMPLE TO LEARN, HARD TO MASTER**
Tap to release the ball, swipe to rotate barriers, fine-tune the angle to let the ball slide and bounce through each stage and drop perfectly into the bucket. One wrong angle and — SMASH!

🌍 **JOURNEY THROUGH 8 WORLDS**
• 🌲 Dense Forest — Wood barriers, basic tutorial
• 🏔️ High Mountains — Glass & rubber materials
• ☁️ Above the Clouds — Zero-gravity & wind fans
• 🌋 Volcano — Moving barriers
• 🌌 Outer Space — Reversed gravity
• 🌕 The Moon — Speed challenges
• 🔴 Mars — Super-hard stages
• ⭐ The Galaxy — Everything combined

🧱 **3 BARRIER MATERIALS**
• 🪵 Wood — Standard friction, easy to control
• 🪟 Glass — Ultra-slippery, ball flies fast (and shatters!)
• 🦿 Rubber — Bouncy and unpredictable!

✨ **KEY FEATURES**
• 35+ hand-crafted levels + infinite procedural generation
• Realistic physics engine (SpriteKit)
• Epic ball shatter effect on miss — flying shards, cracks, dust clouds
• 5 collectible ball skins: Pearl, Neon, Aurora, Fire, Gold
• Score system, personal records & 3-star rating
• Haptics & rich synthesized sound effects
• Auto-detects Vietnamese & English
• Full offline play — no internet required

🎮 **FOR EVERYONE**
Ages 6 and up. Perfect for relaxing, training spatial reasoning, and building problem-solving skills.
