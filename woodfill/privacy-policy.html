🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Duna's Wood Fill ("the App") is a free puzzle game developed by Duna's Studio. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data only on your device using local storage:

  • High score
  • Star count
  • Selected wood type and unlocked wood types
  • Game count

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. We do not have access to it.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads). Google AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

The types of data collected by AdMob may include:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted by user)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data (ad interactions only)

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by:

  • Going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iOS device
  • Declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt when shown

If you opt out, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

Analytics

The App does not use any analytics or crash reporting SDK. No usage data, crash reports, or behavioral data is collected by the developer.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. Ads served are age-appropriate as configured through AdMob's settings.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data. The developer has no access to any of your data.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
