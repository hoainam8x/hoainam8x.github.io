🎮 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop → Premium Store and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your Remove Ads and any coin packs purchased with the same Apple ID.

How do I turn off ads?

Purchase "Remove Ads Forever" for $1.99 in the Premium Store. This removes all banner and interstitial advertisements permanently.

My progress was lost, how do I recover it?

Game progress is saved locally. If you have iCloud enabled, your progress should sync across devices. Unfortunately, we cannot recover locally deleted data.

How do daily missions work?

3 new missions appear each day at midnight. Complete them to earn coins. Missions are the same for all players worldwide each day.

Game Center leaderboards not working?

Make sure you're signed into Game Center in iOS Settings. The game will automatically submit your scores when connected.

How do I change the language?

The game follows your device language. Supported languages: English, Korean (한국어), Japanese (日本語), Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt).

Controls feel unresponsive?

Try adjusting your finger position on the joystick. The game works best with gentle movements rather than rapid tapping.

App crashes or freezes?

Force quit and restart the app. If problems persist, restart your device. Update to the latest iOS version for best performance.

🛠 Troubleshooting

Performance Issues

  • Close other apps running in background
  • Restart your device if the game feels slow
  • Ensure you have at least 100MB free storage
  • Game requires iOS 16.0 or later

Purchase Problems

  • Check your Apple ID has payment method configured
  • Verify purchase in App Store → Account → Purchase History
  • For refunds, contact Apple Support directly
  • Restart app after making purchases

Game Center Issues

  • Sign out and back into Game Center in iOS Settings
  • Check internet connection
  • Achievements may take a few moments to appear

📱 System Requirements
