Privacy Policy for Duna's Neon Tank

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Duna's Neon Tank is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in our iOS game.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifiers: We collect your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) only with your explicit consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Game Analytics: Game progress, scores, levels completed, and gameplay statistics (stored locally on device)
  • Technical Data: Device model, iOS version, app version for performance optimization

Information You Provide

  • Game Center: If you sign in to Game Center, Apple handles your leaderboard and achievement data according to Apple's privacy policy
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase data is processed by Apple through App Store, we only receive confirmation of purchase completion

2. How We Use Information

  • Game Experience: Save your progress, preferences, and achievements locally
  • Personalized Advertising: Show relevant ads through Google AdMob (only with IDFA consent)
  • Performance: Improve game stability and performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can control ad personalization through your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" setting.

Apple Game Center

Game Center integration is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate servers or store personal data on our systems
  • iCloud sync (if enabled) is handled by Apple's secure infrastructure
  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Ad Tracking: You can disable ad personalization in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Data Deletion: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Ad-Free Experience: Purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate advertising
  • Game Center: Disable in iOS Settings → Game Center

6. Children's Privacy

Duna's Neon Tank is rated for ages 9+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game includes:

  • Simulated cartoon violence (tank battles)
  • Optional advertising (can be removed via purchase)
  • In-app purchases with parental controls

7. International Users

This app is available worldwide. Users in the European Union have additional rights under GDPR, including data portability and deletion. Contact us for assistance with these requests.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

This privacy policy complies with Apple App Store requirements, Google AdMob policies, COPPA, and GDPR regulations.
