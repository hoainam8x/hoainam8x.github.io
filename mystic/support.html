❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap two matching tiles to remove them from the board. Tiles must be "free" — not blocked on both left and right sides by other tiles, and not covered by a tile on a higher layer. A valid connecting path (with at most 2 turns) must exist between the two tiles.

What does "Hint" do?

The Hint button highlights a pair of matching tiles that can currently be removed. You start each level with a limited number of hints. Watch a rewarded video to earn extra hints.

What does "Shuffle" do?

Shuffle rearranges the remaining tiles on the board. Use it when you're stuck and can't find any matching pairs. The game also auto-shuffles if no valid moves are available.

Is there a time limit?

Levels 1-15 have no time limit — take your time and relax! Starting from Level 16, a gentle timer is introduced. Higher levels have shorter time limits for an extra challenge. Watch a rewarded video to add 60 seconds.

How many levels are there?

Unlimited! Levels are procedurally generated with increasing difficulty. The game never ends — keep playing to illuminate the Ancient Temple and collect Mystic Fragments.

What is the Temple?

The Temple on the home screen represents your overall journey. As you complete levels and earn Mystic Fragments, the Temple glows brighter. Progress through eras: Dawn → Bloom → Radiance → Twilight → Eternal and beyond!

How do I remove ads?

Currently the app is free and ad-supported. We may introduce an ad-free option in a future update. You can reduce ad frequency by not watching optional rewarded videos.

My game progress was lost. Can you restore it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. Unfortunately, if you delete the app or reset your device, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping your device backed up via iCloud or iTunes. Cloud save may be added in a future update.

The game is running slowly / freezing.

Try the following:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app and iOS
  • If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version

Which devices are supported?

Duna's Mystic Mahjong supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later. The game is optimized for 60 FPS on iPhone 11 and newer.
