Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play the game?

Tap a screw to select it, then tap an empty hole to place it. Remove all screws from a plate and it will fall. Clear all plates to win the level! A tutorial is shown when you first play.

Q: I'm stuck on a level. What can I do?

You have several options:
• Undo — Tap the ↩ button to undo your last move
• Hint — Tap the 💡 button to get a smart suggestion
• Extra Hole — Watch a short video to add an extra hole
• Restart — Pause the game and tap Restart to try again

Q: How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Settings menu (⚙ icon on the main screen) and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Q: How do I remove ads?

You can purchase "Remove Ads" from the in-game store. This permanently removes all banner and interstitial ads. Optional rewarded video ads remain available if you want bonus rewards.

Q: My game progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, your progress is also synced to iCloud. To recover:
• Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device Settings
• Sign in with the same Apple ID
• The game will sync your progress automatically

Q: What are Locked Screws?

Locked screws (shown with a lock icon) need to be tapped multiple times to unlock before you can place them in a hole. The number on the lock shows how many taps are needed.

Q: What are Mystery Holes?

Mystery holes are hidden under plates. You need to remove the plate above them first to reveal the hole. Once revealed, they work like normal holes.

Q: What are Color Holes?

Color holes only accept screws of the matching color. Plan your moves carefully to make sure you place the right color screw in the right hole!

Q: What are Iron Plates?

Iron plates are heavier than wooden plates. They fall faster and can crush wooden plates below them. They're shown with a metallic gray color and bolt marks.

Q: The app is crashing or not working properly.

Please try these steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure your iOS is up to date
4. Reinstall the app (your iCloud progress will be preserved)
If the problem persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

Q: How do I disable haptic feedback or sound?

Tap the ⚙ Settings icon on the main menu. You can toggle Sound and Haptic Feedback on or off.

Q: Is an internet connection required?

No! The game is fully playable offline. All 40+ levels are included in the app. Internet is only needed for ads and iCloud sync.
