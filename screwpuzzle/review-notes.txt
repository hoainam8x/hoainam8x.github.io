# ── Notes for Reviewer ──

Notes:
This is a single-player physics puzzle game. No login or account creation
is required.

HOW TO TEST:
1. Launch the app — you will see the main menu.
2. Tap "PLAY" to enter the level selection screen.
3. Select Level 1 to start playing.
4. Tap a screw (colored circle on a plate) to select it.
5. Tap an empty hole (dark circle at the bottom) to place the screw.
6. When all screws are removed from a plate, it falls with physics.
7. Clear all plates to win the level.

FEATURES TO VERIFY:
- Tutorial appears automatically on Level 1 (first-time players only)
- Undo button reverses the last move (including restoring fallen plates)
- Hint button highlights a suggested move
- Pause button pauses gameplay and shows resume/restart/menu options
- Help (?) button shows level-specific instructions
- Settings (gear icon) toggles sound and haptic feedback
- Win overlay shows stars earned and allows next level/replay/menu
- Lose overlay offers retry, watch ad for extra hole, or return to menu

ADVERTISEMENTS:
- Banner ads appear between levels (level select, win/lose screens)
- Banners are hidden during active gameplay
- Interstitial ads appear after every 3 levels completed (with 60s cooldown)
- Rewarded video ads are optional — shown when player taps "Watch Ad"
  to earn an extra hole during gameplay
- "Remove Ads" IAP removes banner and interstitial ads permanently
- All ads use Google AdMob with proper ATT consent flow

IN-APP PURCHASES:
- "Remove Ads" ($2.99) — removes banner and interstitial ads
- "No Ads + Starter Pack" ($4.99) — removes ads + 500 coins + 10 hints
- "Infinite Lives" ($1.99) — unlimited lives, no waiting
- All purchases are non-consumable and restorable

PRIVACY:
- ATT prompt is shown on first launch (iOS 14.5+)
- No personal data is collected by the app itself
- AdMob may collect IDFA if user consents
- Game progress is stored locally (UserDefaults) and optionally via iCloud
- Full privacy policy: https://dunagame.com/screw-puzzle/privacy

OFFLINE CAPABILITY:
- All 40 levels are bundled with the app
- Game is fully playable without internet
- Internet only needed for ads and iCloud sync

SPECIAL GAME MECHANICS (for reference):
- Locked Screws: Tap 2x to unlock before placing
- Iron Plates: Heavy, fall faster, can crush wooden plates
- Mystery Holes: Hidden under plates, revealed when plate falls
- Color Holes: Only accept matching color screws

This app contains no objectionable content and is rated 4+.
