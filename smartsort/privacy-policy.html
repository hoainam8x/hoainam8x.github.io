Privacy Policy

Duna's Sort Triple Match
Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Nam Vu Hoai ("we", "our", "us") built Duna's Sort Triple Match ("the App") as a free, ad-supported game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Levels played, scores, play time, app crashes
  • Advertising Data: Ad interactions, impressions, clicks (via Google AdMob)

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • ❌ Location data (GPS)
  • ❌ Photos, contacts, or camera access
  • ❌ Payment or financial information
  • ❌ Social media accounts

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

Purpose: Display advertisements to support free gameplay

Data collected: Device identifiers, ad interaction data

Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Analytics (Google)

Purpose: App usage analytics to improve user experience

Data collected: Anonymous usage statistics, crash reports

Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Apple SKAdNetwork

Purpose: Ad attribution without tracking individual users

Data collected: Aggregated, anonymized conversion data

Privacy Policy: developer.apple.com/app-store/ad-attribution

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Our App requests your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can choose to:

  • Allow Tracking: Personalized ads based on your interests
  • Deny Tracking: Non-personalized ads (you still see ads, but they are not targeted)

You can change this setting at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Display advertisements to support free gameplay
  • Analyze app performance and fix crashes
  • Improve game design and level difficulty
  • Ensure fair play

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All game progress is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • Third-party services (Google) handle their data according to their own privacy policies
  • We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties

7. Children's Privacy

Duna's Sort Triple Match is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do NOT require registration or login
  • Ads displayed comply with Google's child-directed content policies
  • If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately

8. Data Retention

  • Local game data: Stored until the user deletes the app
  • Analytics data: Retained by Google according to their retention policies
  • Ad data: Retained by Google AdMob according to their retention policies

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of tracking: Via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Reset advertising identifier: Via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  • Delete all local data: By uninstalling the app
  • Request information: Contact us about what data is collected

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
