🎮 Duna's BlobDrop - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Duna's BlobDrop ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed by DUNA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information directly. The app does not require registration, login, or any personal data to play.

However, the following third-party services may collect certain data:

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center — Provides leaderboards and achievements. Game Center uses your Apple ID. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Stored Locally

The app stores game progress locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • High scores and game statistics
  • Diamond balance and shop purchases
  • Sound preferences (mute on/off)
  • Daily login streak

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. You may see:

  • Banner ads on menu and game over screens
  • Interstitial ads between games
  • Rewarded video ads (opt-in) for in-game bonuses

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads).

5. Children's Privacy

Duna's BlobDrop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal data input.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or databases that store your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
