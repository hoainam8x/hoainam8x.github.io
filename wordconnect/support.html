❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I play the game?

Easy!

  1. Tap letters in the circular board to form words
  2. Swipe through letters in any direction
  3. Find all target words to complete the level
  4. Look for bonus words to earn extra coins! ⭐

The tutorial will guide you through the basics when you first launch the app.

What are coins and how do I use them?

Coins are the game's currency!

  • 💰 Earn coins by finding words (especially bonus words)
  • 🎁 Spend coins on power-ups:
    • Shuffle (10 coins): Rearrange letters
    • Reveal Letter (20 coins): Show one letter of a word
    • Hint (15 coins): Get direction guidance

How do stars work?

Stars measure how well you completed a level!

  • ⭐ 1 Star: Completed the level
  • ⭐⭐ 2 Stars: Found most words with few hints
  • ⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars: Perfect! Found all words with minimal hints

You can replay levels to improve your star rating!

How can I see word definitions?

Tap any word you've found!

  • Tap a word in the "Found Words" panel
  • See English definition and Vietnamese translation (if available)
  • Tap the speaker icon to hear pronunciation

Note: Definitions use public dictionary services (Wiktionary, Free Dictionary API, and optional machine translation) and require an internet connection. Checking whether a swipe is a valid English bonus word uses a built-in offline word list.

What are achievements?

Unlock achievements by reaching milestones!

  • 🏆 12 achievements to unlock
  • Track your progress in the Achievements panel
  • Examples: Word Wizard (find 100+ words), Speed Reader (complete level under 2 min)

Tap the trophy icon (🏆) to see all achievements.

I'm stuck on a level. What should I do?

Don't worry, here are some tips:

  • 💡 Use the Hint button - It shows the direction of a word
  • 🔀 Try Shuffle - Sometimes seeing letters in a different order helps
  • 📝 Try different combinations - Don't just look for common words
  • 🔄 Look for reversed words - Words can be spelled backwards too!
  • ⭐ Hunt for bonus words - They give extra coins and points

My game progress was lost! Can you help?

Unfortunately, game progress is stored locally on your device.

Progress can be lost if you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Reset your device
  • Restore your device from a backup that doesn't include app data

Prevention tip: Enable iCloud backup for your device (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup).

We're working on cloud save features for a future update! 🚀

The app crashes or freezes. What can I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force quit the app: Swipe up from the bottom and swipe the app away
  2. Restart your device: Turn it off and on again
  3. Update the app: Check the App Store for updates
  4. Update iOS: Go to Settings → General → Software Update
  5. Reinstall the app: Delete and download again (⚠️ will lose progress)

If the problem persists, please email us at support@duna.app with:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (e.g., iOS 17.2)
  • What you were doing when it crashed
  • Screenshots if possible

Can I play offline?

Yes! ✅

  • All 65 levels work offline
  • Game progress is saved locally
  • Achievements and coins work offline

What needs internet:

  • Word definitions (Wiktionary, dictionaryapi.dev, translation helper when you open the definition sheet)
  • Advertisements (Google AdMob)

How do I turn off sound/haptics?

Currently, sound and haptics follow your device settings.

  • Mute sound: Use the physical mute switch on your iPhone
  • Disable haptics: Settings → Sounds & Haptics → System Haptics (toggle off)

We're planning to add in-app settings in a future update!
