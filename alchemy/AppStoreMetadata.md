### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
🧪 Welcome to Duna's Alchemy Master — the most enchanting potion-sorting puzzle game!

Step into a mystical alchemist's laboratory where colorful magical potions await your expertise. Pour, sort, and organize bubbling liquids into gleaming alchemy flasks. Can you master every concoction?

✨ HOW TO PLAY
• Tap a flask to select it, then tap another to pour
• Pour liquid only into empty flasks or flasks with the same color on top
• Complete a level by sorting all colors into separate flasks
• Simple to learn, challenging to master!

🔮 MAGICAL FEATURES
• 25 handcrafted levels + infinite procedurally generated levels
• Unique alchemy-themed mechanics:
  ❄️ Ice Blocks — Frozen layers that must be thawed before moving
  ❓ Mystery Potions — Hidden colors revealed only when uncovered
  💔 Cracked Flasks — Limited pours before they shatter!
  🛢️ Oil Layers — Always float to the top, blocking your moves

🧙‍♂️ BOOSTER MAGIC
• Undo — Reverse your last move (3 free per level!)
• Add Flask — Create an extra empty flask when you're stuck
• Magic Wand — Extract any layer from the middle of a flask
• Reset — Start the level fresh with a new strategy

🎨 STUNNING VISUALS
• Gorgeous glossy alchemy flasks with realistic glass reflections
• Vivid, saturated potion colors with liquid-like rendering
• Deep magical purple-blue backgrounds with twinkling stars
• Smooth animations and satisfying visual effects

🎵 IMMERSIVE SOUND
• Procedurally generated sound effects — every pour, splash, and clink is unique
• Ambient background music for a relaxing puzzle experience
• Satisfying audio feedback for every interaction

🏆 ENDLESS CHALLENGE
• Levels 1-25: Carefully handcrafted with increasing difficulty
• Levels 26+: Infinite procedurally generated puzzles that scale in difficulty
• Every generated level is verified solvable by our built-in AI solver
• New mechanics introduced gradually as you progress

📱 DESIGNED FOR ALL DEVICES
• Optimized for iPhone and iPad
• Supports Dynamic Island, notch, and all screen sizes
• Safe area compliance for comfortable one-handed play
• Portrait mode for easy accessibility

Free to play with optional rewarded ads for bonus items. No pay-to-win mechanics — skill is all you need!

Download now and begin your alchemy adventure! 🧪✨
```
