📖 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play the game?

Tap on the mutant bugs as they appear on screen to SPLAT them! Each bug type has different behaviors — some need multiple taps, some split into smaller bugs, and some are super fast. Kill bugs to earn Bio-Gems and climb the waves!

Q: What are Bio-Gems used for?

Bio-Gems are the in-game currency. Spend them in the Upgrade Shop to improve your abilities: Tap Damage, Splat Radius, Gem Bonus, and Slow Spawn. Access the shop from the main menu.

Q: Can I play offline?

Yes! The game works fully offline. You just won't see ads (which means no ad-based rewards like revive or bonus gems).

Q: How does the Revive feature work?

When you die, you get one chance to revive by watching a short rewarded video ad. This gives you 1 extra life to continue your run. You can only revive once per game.

Q: How do I remove ads?

A premium "Remove Ads" option may be available as an in-app purchase in future updates.

Q: My game progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, progress cannot be recovered. Cloud save is planned for a future update.

Q: The game is laggy or crashes. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure your iOS is updated to version 16.0 or later
  • Reinstall the app (note: progress will be lost)

If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

Q: What devices are supported?

SPLAT Bio-Hazard supports all iPhones and iPads running iOS 16.0 or later. This includes iPhone 8 and newer, iPad (5th generation) and newer.

Q: How do I stop personalized ads?

When the app first launches, you'll see an "Allow Tracking" prompt. Choose "Ask App Not to Track" to disable personalized ads. You can also change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
