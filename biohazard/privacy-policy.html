🔒 Privacy Policy

SPLAT Bio-Hazard ("the App", "we", "us", or "our")

Effective Date: March 31, 2026 · Last Updated: March 31, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do NOT require account creation or login
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or address
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • We do NOT collect location data

b) Information Collected by Third-Party Services:

The App uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device Identifiers: IDFA (if you grant permission via App Tracking Transparency)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, screen size, language
  • Usage Data: App interactions, ad views, ad clicks
  • IP Address: For approximate geographic location (country/city level)

See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

c) Locally Stored Data:

  • Game progress (high score, gems, upgrades) is stored locally via UserDefaults
  • This data never leaves your device

2. How We Use Information

  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized ad performance metrics
  • Game Progress: Local storage for saving your progress

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5+, we request permission before tracking across apps.

  • "Allow": Personalized ads via IDFA
  • "Ask App Not to Track": Non-personalized ads only

Change anytime: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • SKAdNetwork — Apple's privacy-preserving ad attribution

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is rated 9+ for Infrequent/Mild Cartoon Violence.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Stored until you delete the App
  • AdMob data: Per Google's retention policy

To delete all data: uninstall the App.

7. Data Security

Local data is sandboxed by iOS. We do not operate servers or databases.

8. Your Rights

All users:

  • Right to opt-out of tracking via ATT or Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising
  • Right to delete data by uninstalling the App

California (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. Opt out of targeted ads via ATT.

EU (GDPR): Legal basis is consent (ATT prompt). Withdraw anytime via device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
